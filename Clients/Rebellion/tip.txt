Being the best is more than just being able to attack quickly.
Be careful in the borderlands! The opposing forces can swiftly ambush you.
You can only change sides after you delete all of your existing characters.
You can move using 'w', 'a', 's', 'd' (double-tap 'w' to auto-run) or by clicking your mouse.
Ultimate Mode characters are stronger than Hard (Basic) Mode characters, but they require more EXP to level up.
Double-tap 'q', 'e' and 's' to launch into rolls and flips. Press 'c' to sit down and take a rest.
If you get stuck, press Space-Bar to jump, or type '/return' in normal chat.
